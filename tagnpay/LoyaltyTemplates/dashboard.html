Analytics

Analytics refers to the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data from mall customers loyalty programs to gain insights into customer behavior, program effectiveness, and overall business performance. By examining how customers interact with a mall's loyalty offerings, businesses can optimize their programs to increase customer retention, improve sales, and enhance customer satisfaction.
